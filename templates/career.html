'GIT' MORE

Taking Stock

I used convolutional neural networks to categorize inventory images into product groups, resulting in a handy tool to streamline online inventory systems. I also used clustering to group images of similar items and dimension reduction to visualize the variety of products within categories and sub-categories.

'GIT' MORE

Outfox a Fraud

I built a model for identifying fraudulent events posted to an online ticket vendor. I engineered features to extract quantitative data from text. I compared model performance across random forest, logistic regression, and gradient boosting models, using hyperparameter tuning for optimization.

'GIT' MORE

Elections for Sale?

I examined the correlation of campaign spending and election results using regression analysis. To minimize external sources of variation, I focus specifically on instances of rematches between the same two candidates for U.S. House seats.
